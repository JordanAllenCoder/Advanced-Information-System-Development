Event Information

Flag Football Funday's: Flag football fundays are held every sunday at NRG stadium. Flag football fundays are for all genders of all ages. We are an inclusive football club that believes that everybody should be able to play the sport that they love. We will be out there every sunday from 12 PM to 6 PM with 4 different sections to host games. We have concession stands with all of your favorite snacks and the coldest drinks in town. Complete the signup form and come get your flag football funday on this sunday at NRG stadium!
